Wild Rice Soup

  1/4 cup olive oil
  1 large yellow onion, diced
  2-3 large carrots, diced
  10oz white mushrooms sliced
  3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  1-2 tsp salt + more as needed
  1/2 tsp black pepper
  1 tsp dried thyme
  2 tbsp corestarch, arrowroot starch, or tapioca starch
  1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  3 + 1/4 cups vegetable broth
  1/2 cup water + more as needed
  2 tbsp coconut aminos or tamari
  1 cup wild rice, rinsed
  1 large cup shredded kale
  1/2 15oz can full fat coconut milk, shaken

Steps:
1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in large pot, add diced onion and carrots
2. Cook onion and carrots, stirring periodically, for 10 mins or until onions are translicent
3. Add sliced mushrooms and minced garlic, stir and cook for 10 mins
4. Mix in salt, black pepper, and thyme let cook 3-5 mins
5. Add Starch and mix well, do same with yeast
6. Pour in veg broth, 1/2 cup water, aminos and wild rice. Put lid on pot and bring to boil
7. Once boiling, reduce heat to medium-low and cook covered for 40-55 mins, stirring ocasionally
8. Once rice is fully cooked add in kale/greens and coconut milk
